About Me

Just a little bit about myself...


I started my professional career what feels like a lifetime ago. I began working in an auto garage as a mechanic, so that I could earn enough to put myself through school. However, six years later, I found myself at the top of my profession doing everything from maintenance and service, to diagnostics and complete engine rebuilds as a certified master technician. But after a physical injury and multiple surgeries later, I was medically advised to discontinue my work as a technican. Not sure what I was going to do next, I took my college degree and began to seek other opportunites in the big wide world.

I eventually found myself with an interest in software engineering and development, as the Product Anaylsis of a ColdFusion development team at a local ad agency here in STL. From there I worked side-by-side with many talented developers that encouraged me to pursue my interest in becoming a developer. So now I am learning more than I ever thought possible, and loving every moment of it!
